Somethings about me

I am a programmer that loves solving problems and facing new challenges. I have a big passion for videogames which has made me become a game developer where I can put in practice my programming skills to create games. I see videogames as universes where we can define the rules and I can put my creativity as a developer to generate experiences.

As a gamer I have found not only joy but admiration for videogames which gave me the motivation to be a game developer.

When I am not working I like to do courses that make me better at what I do, to be up to date on the latest game news and one of my passions is to play bass guitar.
